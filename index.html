KFC

Kentucky Fried Chicken, commonly known as KFC, is a globally renowned fast-food chain celebrated for its signature fried chicken. Founded by Colonel Harland Sanders in 1952, KFC boasts a secret blend of 11 herbs and spices that give its chicken its iconic flavor. With a menu ranging from classic buckets of chicken to sandwiches, wraps, and sides like mashed potatoes and coleslaw, KFC offers a delicious taste of Southern comfort food. With thousands of locations worldwide, KFC continues to satisfy cravings with its crispy, flavorful chicken served fresh and hot.
